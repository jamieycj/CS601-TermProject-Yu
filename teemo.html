Let's talk about Teemo

How we got Teemo?

Teemo is actually a guest to our home. We will take care of him for a year while his owner, our two best friends settle down in Texas. Teemo loves to hang out in our home, sometimes in our neighbours' too. He tries to escape and take some fresh air every time the front door is opened. His most accomplished escape plan includes a 2-hour missing and being an uninvited guest to a neighbour's yard that is 5 lots apart from us.
