Let's talk about Sam

How we got Sam?

Sam was born at an English Creme Golden Retriever breeder's farm in Arlington, Washington. He was 12 weeks old, weighted 15 pounds when we brought him home. During the selection process, there were 2 litters, over 20 puppies for us to choose from and we were indecisive. Sam was the only one who didn't return to kennel when the whole litter were told to. He instead walked to our side, sat down and looked at us with innocent puppy eyes. That was the moment we knew that we were bound to be a family. As the breeder said, Sam chose us.


Why named Sam?

Sam is the name of one of the archangels. He is an angel to us.
